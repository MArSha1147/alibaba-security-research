Theme 1

MetaCompress – A Learning-Based Token Compression Approach

We introduce MetaCompress, a novel learning-based token reduction method that formulates visual token compression as a learnable mapping to replace suboptimal heuristic designs like attention scores. By employing a data-efficient training paradigm, MetaCompress achieves superior efficiency-accuracy trade-offs and robust generalization across multi-turn Visual Question Answering scenarios for Large Vision-Language Models.

Theme 2

RiskGen-Enhance – A Multimodal Generation-Augmented Framework for Risk-Oriented Visual Understanding

In the context of risk management business practices at Alibaba Group, we have observed that multimodal generation yields substantial improvements in high-difficulty, fine-grained risk visual understanding capabilities. Motivated by these observations, we have integrated image generation techniques into the training pipeline of visual understanding models, with three key contributions:

  • Incorporating spatial understanding priors into the training process to enhance geometric and structural comprehension; (CVPR 2026 SIMPLEPOSTER)
  • Accelerate diffusion process through inference-time reasoning process refinement; (CVPR 2026 TC-Pade)
  • Pioneering the proposal of a diffusion probe to identify and filter unavailable synthesized samples during large-scale data production (CVPR 2026 Diffusion Probe)

These research efforts collectively serve as a critical foundation for the large-scale, high-throughput adversarial framework currently under development.
